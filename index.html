Projects

Fantasy Football Statistics

Description

I started playing fantasy football with some coworkers back in 2016. A year or two into playing, another (non-sports loving) coworker in the office started asking questions about what all the numbers meant. He developed a system to determine how well each team did each week at setting their lineups. I was intrigured and soon I had a whole spreadsheet set up that tracked multiple statistics about each team.

While the spreadsheet was a good place to start, it wasn't particuarly flexible so when the league grew to 12 teams, it broke. It was about that time that my new company started to switch to React and Typescript, neither of which I had any experience with. I decided to take the opportunity to learn these and build a new fantasy football statistics tracker.

Hence, this project. It's still a work in progress and I have some high hopes for it.
